Chapter One

Our Story

Seven years of laughter, adventures, and quiet moments — written one chapter at a time.

01 · Where it began
campus library · 2019
i.The Meeting
March · 2019

A borrowed pen, a longer conversation.

It was supposed to be a five-minute favor in the campus library. Three hours later, the librarian was politely turning off the lights and neither of us had noticed.

Universitas Indonesia
02 · First date
kemang · rainy evening
ii.The First Date
May · 2019

Coffee that turned into dinner, then a long walk home.

A small café in Kemang, an unexpected downpour, and one umbrella between us. We laughed the whole way back. Neither of us slept much that night.

Kemang, Jakarta
03 · First trip
ubud · sunrise
iii.Our First Adventure
December · 2020

Sunrise on a rice terrace, and a quiet certainty.

Bali in the off-season. Empty roads, warm rain, and one morning where the world felt entirely ours. We took a picture neither of us has ever shared with anyone.

Tegallalang, Bali
04 · Moving in
our first apartment
iv.Building a Home
August · 2022

One tiny apartment, two very loud opinions on lamps.

We argued about paint colors, bought a couch that wouldn't fit through the door, and learned that home isn't a place — it's whoever's already there when you get back.

Tebet, Jakarta Selatan
05 · The proposal
nusa penida · golden hour
v.The Question
February · 2025

A cliff, a sunset, and one impossibly long pause.

He rehearsed the whole speech for weeks. Then the wind picked up, the ring almost flew away, and all that came out was "so… yes?" She said yes anyway. Twice.

Nusa Penida
06 · Today
together · today
vi.The Next Chapter
November · 2026

And now, the best part — together, for good.

Seven years to get here, and somehow it still feels like the beginning. We can't wait to start the next chapter with you in the room. See you in November.
